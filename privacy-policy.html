Privacy Policy

Finio · Effective date: 2026-04-09 · Last updated: 2026-04-29

This Privacy Policy applies to the Finio mobile application (the "Application"), developed by Tomasz Mancewicz (the "Service Provider"). Finio is a freemium personal finance application provided "AS IS".

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account data: email address and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Financial data: transaction records, account balances, budget envelopes, categories, and saving goals that you enter manually or via receipt scanning.
  • Receipt images: photos of receipts you submit for AI-powered scanning. These images are transmitted to Google Gemini (see Section 4) for processing and are not stored permanently by the Service Provider.
  • Household data: if you create or join a household group, your financial data is shared with other members of that household.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, IP address, and app usage data collected automatically for service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Application and its features.
  • To process receipt images using AI to extract transaction data on your behalf.
  • To share financial data within your household group as you have configured.
  • To send important service notifications (e.g. account security alerts).
  • To improve the Application based on aggregated, anonymized usage data.

3. Household Data Sharing

Finio allows you to create or join a household group. When you are part of a household, your financial data (transactions, accounts, budgets, categories) is visible to all members of that household. You should only join households with people you trust. Leaving a household does not automatically delete shared data — the household owner retains the data.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that process your data under their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and backend infrastructure. Your account and financial data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini — AI model used to extract data from receipt photos. When you scan a receipt, the image is transmitted to Google Gemini for processing. Images are not stored by the Service Provider after processing. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

The Service Provider does not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Automatically collected technical data is retained for up to 24 months. If you delete your account, your personal data and financial records are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Some aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for analytical purposes.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Export: download your data directly from the Application (Settings → Export data).
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: delete your account and all associated data directly from the Application (Settings → Delete account).
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app controls or contact us at support.finio@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately at support.finio@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and row-level security on the database. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: support.finio@gmail.com
