Account Deletion – Finio

Last updated: April 9, 2026

You can delete your Finio account and all associated data directly from within the app. This page explains how to do it and what data is removed.

How to delete your account

  • Open the Finio app
  • Go to Settings
  • Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account
  • Confirm the action in the dialog
Important: If you are the owner of a household with other members, you must transfer ownership or remove all members before deleting your account.

What gets deleted

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • All transactions, accounts, budgets, envelopes and saving goals associated with your household
  • Your household and all its data (if you are the sole member)
  • All locally stored session data on your device

What is retained

  • No personal data is retained after deletion
  • Backups are purged within 30 days
